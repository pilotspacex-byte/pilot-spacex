## Model Calibration Notes

### Opus 4.5 Calibration

Templates are calibrated for Opus 4.5 to avoid overtriggering. Key adjustments:

| Aggressive (avoid) | Calibrated (used) |
|--------------------|-------------------|
| `CRITICAL: You MUST...` | `This is important to...` |
| `ALWAYS do X` | `Do X` |
| `NEVER skip...` | `Don't skip...` |
| `Think step by step` | `Evaluate step by step` |

### Other Models

For GPT-4, Sonnet, or other models, you may increase instruction intensity:
- Add "MUST" and "CRITICAL" for emphasis
- Use "Think step by step" (standard CoT phrasing)
- Add "NO EXCEPTIONS" for strict constraints
