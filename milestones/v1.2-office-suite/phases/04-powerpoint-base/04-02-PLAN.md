    - Each thumbnail slot has a wrapper div

    - When a slot becomes visible (intersects), render a small `<canvas>` and call pptxviewjs to render that slide at thumbnail size
    - Cache rendered thumbnails — once rendered, don't re-render on scroll
    - Use a `Set<number>` or `Map<number, boolean>` ref to track which slides have been rendered

    - Render each visible slide to its own small canvas element.
    - Canvas size: 120px width, height calculated from slide dimensions.

    **Auto-scroll to active:**
